## About

I'm an **Embedded Systems & Software Developer** based in Galway, Ireland, focused on designing efficient, real-world solutions. From low-power medical devices to IoT products, I work across hardware and software to bring ideas into working prototypes and products — hands-on, practical, and driven by curiosity.

## Experience

### Product Systems Engineer — Galenband
*Galway, Ireland · Since November 2023*

Designing and prototyping a Class II FDA medical device for atrial fibrillation (Afib) detection, acquiring Lead I equivalent ECG signals from the upper left arm. I architect ultra-low-power hardware with discrete power management, design high-density PCBs (up to 8 layers), and collaborate with firmware and mechanical teams on the full system. I also build Python automation tools and internal web portals for ECG data analysis, demos, and validation.

### Embedded Systems Engineer — TIF Labs
*Bangalore, India · January 2020 – May 2023*

Designed 20+ microcontroller PCBs, sensor boards, and full products. Led R&D for products including Alpha Care, IOTIF Trainer Kit, and the WittyFox lineup. Directed the product lifecycle for 10+ new products, managed a team of engineers, and delivered client projects as a technical consultant. Also built Python tools, RPA automation for hospitals, and hosted technical IoT workshops.
